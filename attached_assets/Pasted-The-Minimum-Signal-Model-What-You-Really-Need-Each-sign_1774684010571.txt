Medium

Direction Must Be Explicit

Every signal must answer:

Does this push the outcome forward or hold it back?

Allowed directions:

Supports outcome
Slows outcome
Neutral
Unclear

That is all.

Confidence Must Be Explicit

Every signal must answer:

How reliable is the evidence?

Allowed levels:

Strong
Moderate
Weak
