What went wrong (diagnosis)

The system defaulted to a generic pattern:

"RFP → choose agency"

But this specific document is:

"RFP → solve a launch marketing problem"

Those are very different decision classes.

The error is not semantic — it is ontological.

The system picked the wrong decision archetype.
