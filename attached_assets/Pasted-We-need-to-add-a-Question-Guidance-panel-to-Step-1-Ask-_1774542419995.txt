We need to add a Question Guidance panel to Step 1 — Ask a Question.

Purpose:
Help users see how strong strategic questions are structured and prevent vague or untestable inputs.

This is a UI-only feature.
No engine changes required.

---

PLACEMENT

Step 1 — Ask a Question

Add a right-side panel titled:

Question Examples

---

SECTION 1 — Strong Strategic Questions

Add a section titled:

Strong Strategic Questions

Display example questions as clickable chips or cards.

Examples:

* Will adoption increase after indication expansion within 12 months?
* Which segment is most likely to adopt first after launch?
* Will payer restrictions delay uptake in the first year?
* Will guideline endorsement shift prescribing behavior?
* Will competitor entry materially reduce share within 6 months?

Behavior:

Clicking a question should populate the main question input field.

---

SECTION 2 — Avoid These Questions

Add a section titled:

Avoid These Questions

Display example questions styled differently (muted or warning color).

Examples:

* What will happen with this product?
* Is this launch going to be successful?
* What do doctors think?
* How will the market react?
* Will this work?

These examples should not populate the field automatically.
They are instructional only.

---

VISUAL DESIGN

Use two stacked sections:

Strong Strategic Questions

Avoid These Questions

Use color distinction:

Good examples:

green or neutral

orange or muted red

Keep the panel compact and readable.
