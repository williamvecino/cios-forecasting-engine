Case 1 — Regulatory Risk Archetype
Cardiovascular Safety / Label Restriction Risk
Case name

Xarelto GI Bleeding Regulatory Risk

Company / Product
Company: Bayer / Johnson & Johnson
Product: Xarelto
Class: Direct oral anticoagulant
Forecast question

Will U.S. regulators impose a new gastrointestinal bleeding warning or label restriction for Xarelto within the next 12 months?

Decision domain

Regulatory safety risk

Time horizon

12 months

Decision archetype

Regulatory risk / pharmacovigilance escalation

What this case is really about

Whether accumulating safety signals and regulatory attention are strong enough to trigger a formal label change or restriction.

This is not about:

physician adoption
comparative efficacy
marketing positioning

It is about:

regulatory action probability

safety signal detection or disproportionality analysis

regulatory inspection or inquiry

risk-benefit reassessment activity
litigation or pharmacovigilance escalation

guideline updates unrelated to safety
marketing campaigns
physician preference

brand perception

rivaroxaban + safety signal

rivaroxaban + pharmacovigilance
